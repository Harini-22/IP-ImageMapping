1.HARIYALI CHOLE



           The addition of green chutney in chole makes it special. They are available at every small restaurant in Punjab. It is always served with parantha. This is one of the famous dishes in Punjab.



2.CHANA MASALA



           As the name of the dish is suggesting the taste of the dish. Masala Chana is full of spices which give it a totally different touch and taste as well. The specialty of this chole is that the recipe of this chole is a little bit tough but the taste is just out of the world. It is always served with bhature. This is one of the famous dishes in Punjab.



3.PALAK CHOLE



           Palak Chole is usually found in every place of India. It is always served with rice. They are very spicy. The special part of this dish is that it is a combination of taste and health benefit as well. This is one of the famous dishes in Punjab. It is well garnished with chopped coriander leaves and parsley leaves along with onion slices and green chili as well.
